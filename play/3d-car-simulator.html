3d Car Simulator

Fullscreen

Experience the thrill of high-speed driving and explore a virtual world filled with endless possibilities in the 3D Car Simulator game. With its stunning graphics and realistic physics, this game puts you behind the wheel of powerful vehicles as you navigate through city streets, highways, and off-road terrain. Whether you're a speed demon looking for adrenaline-pumping action or simply want to cruise around and admire the scenery, 3D Car Simulator offers an immersive driving experience like no other.

Game Guide

Here's a guide to the controls in 3D Car Simulator:

  • WASD or arrow keys to drive
  • Space bar to use handbrake
  • C to change camera
  • Shift to change car
  • R to reset car
  • E to turn on/off police car's lights

Mastering these controls will allow you to navigate the open world with ease, whether you're cruising through the city, drifting around corners, or engaging in high-speed pursuits. So buckle up, rev your engines, and get ready to experience the ultimate driving adventure in 3D Car Simulator!
