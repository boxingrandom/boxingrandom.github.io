Gunspin

Fullscreen

Embark on an exhilarating adventure in the wild west with "GunSpin," a captivating browser game that challenges your skill and precision. Set in the rugged desert landscape, GunSpin offers a unique gameplay experience where you use the recoil of your gun to navigate through obstacles and reach your destination.

How to Play GunSpin

In this skill-based physics game, utilize your gun's recoil to propel it across the rocky desert terrain. Watch the dotted line to see where your gun is aiming as it spins through the air. Fire bullets when your gun points left, away from your desired direction of travel. Be cautious not to fire bullets in the wrong direction, risking loss of speed or even backward propulsion.
